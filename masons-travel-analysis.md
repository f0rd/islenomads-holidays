# Mason's Travel Website Analysis

## Design Style & Color Scheme
- **Primary Colors**: Dark Navy Blue (#1a3a52 or similar), Bright Lime Green (#00FF00 or similar)
- **Accent Colors**: Teal/Turquoise (from island imagery)
- **Typography**: Elegant serif font for headings, clean sans-serif for body
- **Overall Aesthetic**: Professional, premium, modern with nature-inspired elements

## Key Layout Features

### Hero Section
- Full-width background image (beach/island scene)
- Large, elegant serif headline overlaid on image
- Tagline/subheading with accent color
- Statistics section below hero (years, clients, partners)
- CTA buttons with green accent color

### Navigation
- Fixed/sticky header with dark background
- Logo on left, navigation menu on right
- Search functionality integrated
- Clean, minimal design

### Content Sections
1. **"What We Do"** - 6 service cards with icons and descriptions
   - Seychelles Specialist
   - Transfers
   - Weddings + Events
   - Unique Excursions
   - Private VIP Tours
   - Cruiseship Handling

2. **"Why Book With Us"** - 4 benefit cards with headings and descriptions
   - Wealth of Experience
   - Deep Local Knowledge
   - Outstanding Personalised Service
   - Commitment to Innovation

3. **Image Gallery** - Grid of high-quality travel photos with overlays
4. **Newsletter Signup** - Email subscription section
5. **Footer** - Comprehensive footer with links and info

## Visual Elements
- Large, high-quality imagery throughout
- Green accent color used for buttons, links, and highlights
- Icons for service categories
- Statistics displayed prominently
- Dashed borders/lines in green as design accents
- Professional photography showcasing destinations

## Typography
- Serif fonts for main headings (elegant, premium feel)
- Sans-serif for body text (readability)
- Good contrast and hierarchy
- Generous whitespace

## Key Sections to Replicate
1. Hero with statistics overlay
2. Service offerings grid (6 items)
3. Why choose us section (4 benefit cards)
4. Image gallery with grid layout
5. Newsletter signup
6. Comprehensive footer
7. Green accent color throughout for CTAs and highlights

## Unique Features
- Professional DMC positioning
- Emphasis on experience and local knowledge
- Strong social proof (years in business, clients served, partners)
- Multiple service offerings clearly organized
- High-quality, professional imagery
