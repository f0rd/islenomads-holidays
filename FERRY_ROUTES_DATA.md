# Maldives Ferry Routes & Pricing Data

## Popular Ferry Routes with Details

### Route 309: Male to Maafushi (Most Popular)
- **Origin**: Male (Ibrahim Nasir International Airport Terminal)
- **Destination**: Maafushi
- **Distance**: ~30 km
- **Travel Time**: 90 minutes
- **Price**: MVR 22 ($1.50 USD)
- **Frequency**: Daily except Friday
- **Vessel Type**: Traditional Dhoni Ferry (wooden boat)
- **Operator**: MTCC
- **Capacity**: ~50-80 passengers
- **Amenities**: Basic seating, toilets, no air-conditioning
- **Stops**: Gulhi (45 min), Maafushi (90 min)

## Pricing Summary

### Price Ranges by Distance
- **Short routes (30-45 min)**: MVR 22-30 ($1.50-$2 USD)
- **Medium routes (45-90 min)**: MVR 30-50 ($2-$3.35 USD)
- **Long routes (90+ min)**: MVR 50-450 ($3.35-$30 USD)

### Speedboat Alternatives
- **Scheduled Speedboat**: $20-$25 USD per person (60 minutes typical)
- **Private Speedboat Charter**: $150-$250 USD per person (45 minutes)
- **Resort Speedboat**: $300+ USD per person

## Ferry Operator Information

### MTCC (Maldives Transport and Contracting Company)
- **Status**: Government-operated public service
- **Routes**: 63 ferry routes with 187 ferry stations
- **Service Area**: Entire archipelago
- **Hotline**: 1650
- **Website**: https://mtcc.mv/
- **Booking Portal**: https://rtl.mv/
- **Vessel Type**: Traditional wooden dhoni ferries
- **Capacity**: 40-100+ passengers per vessel

### Private Operators
- **Naseeb Express**: Himmafushi-Male route with speedboat service
- **Various speedboat operators**: Private charter services

## Ferry Schedule Patterns

### Operating Days
- Most ferries: 3-6 times per week
- **CLOSED**: Every Friday (Islamic holiday)
- Popular routes: Daily except Friday

### Typical Departure Times
- Morning: 06:00-08:00
- Midday: 10:00-12:00
- Afternoon: 14:00-16:00
- Evening: 18:00-20:00

### Schedule Variability
- Schedules change seasonally (Ramadan, holidays)
- Weather can affect schedules
- No advance booking available
- Tickets purchased at terminal on departure day

## Ferry Experience Details

### Vessel Characteristics
- **Design**: Traditional wooden dhoni boats
- **Length**: Typically 60 feet
- **Propulsion**: Motor-powered
- **Seating**: Plastic chairs and bench seating
- **Facilities**: Basic toilets, limited shelter
- **Cargo**: Mixed passenger/freight configuration

### Passenger Experience
- Authentic local atmosphere
- Travel with Maldivian residents
- No air-conditioning (ventilation only)
- Journey times: 1.5-6 hours depending on route
- Cultural immersion experience

## Data Collection Notes

- Most comprehensive schedule information available from MTCC official website
- Guesthouses on local islands often have more current schedules than online sources
- Prices in MVR (Maldivian Rufiyaa) - approximately MVR 15 = $1 USD
- No advance booking system - tickets purchased same-day at terminal
- Payment in cash only (MVR or USD accepted)
